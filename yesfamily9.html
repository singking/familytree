Underlying data source

This tree is ultimately constructed from data entered in a spreadsheet having several mandatory columns:
  1. ID - must be unique
  2. Mother ID - references another row who is the mother
  3. Father ID - references another row who is the father
  4. First Name
  5. Middle Name
  6. Family Name
  7. Other columns are gravey, they provide additional person information

It's a work in progress so nobody has been deliberately left out. If you can help expand or fix any inaccuracies get in touch - doesn't matter about the direction or who you want to add.
